Guy's Hobbies

Originally only playing as a pastime, Guy now plays paintball on a semi-competitive basis. Every Sunday Guy meets with his team to practice before participating in an invitational or tournament match.

Guy enjoys spending time with his family whether it's at home or out about town. Just about every trip that he plans involves his family. Guy wants his family to have plenty of memories and experiences throughout their lives.

Working as an Operations Manager of Inventory Control, Guy aspires to open his own business as a Supply Chain Consultant.
